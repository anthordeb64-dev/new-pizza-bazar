Our Story

Born in the Heart of Dhaka

Pizza Bazar started with one dream: bring the true taste of Naples to Bangladesh. Our founder spent years learning the art of Neapolitan pizza-making before opening our first small kitchen in Dhaka.

Today, we serve thousands of happy customers every week. But one thing has never changed — every pizza that leaves our kitchen is made by hand, with love, in our authentic wood-fired oven.

We source the freshest local ingredients and combine them with imported Italian essentials to create something truly special — a pizza that tells our story in every slice.
