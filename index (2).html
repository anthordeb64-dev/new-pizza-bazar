The Pizza Bazar Story

Born in the vibrant streets of Dhaka, Pizza Bazar started with one simple dream: to bring the unadulterated, bold taste of authentic pizza to Bangladesh.

We don't believe in fast-food shortcuts. Our dough undergoes a 48-hour slow fermentation process. Our rich tomato sauce is crafted daily from scratch. And our pizzas are blistered to perfection in real wood-fired style ovens.

Whether you're grabbing a slice on the go or settling in for a feast, prepare for an unforgettable crunch.
